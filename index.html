An Integrated Responsible Product Development Framework for Impact Tech AIoT Startups

A 3D Framework: Product Lifecycle × Responsible Innovation × Five Core Dimensions

Cristal Rivera Picado  |  Tampere University  |  2025

3D Framework Structure

  • Product Development Lifecycle (6 stages — the “spine”)
  • Responsible Innovation Principles (4 lenses for decision-making)
  • Five Core Dimensions (sustainability, accessibility, SDGs, technology, viability)

Responsible Innovation Principles

👁 Anticipation

Look ahead — what might happen? System thinking about all implications. Acknowledge complexities and uncertainties.

💡 Reflexivity

Look inward — what assumptions are we making? Critically examine own assumptions, values, theories. Recognize limits of knowledge.

💬 Inclusion

Look around — who should be involved? Involve stakeholders and public early via dialogue. Substantive decision-making and mutual learning.

⚡ Responsiveness

Look forward — how will we adapt? Capacity to change direction based on stakeholder values. Adjust while recognizing insufficient knowledge.

How to Use This Framework

  1. Not all questions need to be answered at every stage
  2. Questions are prompts for discussion, not compliance checklists
  3. Prioritize based on your context and stage
  4. Document your responses for iteration and stakeholder communication
  5. Revisit regularly as you learn and circumstances change
